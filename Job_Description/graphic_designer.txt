Graphic Designer Job Description
Job Overview:
We are looking for a creative and detail-oriented Graphic Designer to join our team. The successful candidate will be responsible for creating visually appealing designs that communicate our brand message across various digital and print media.

Key Responsibilities:

Develop and design layouts, graphics, and illustrations for websites, advertisements, brochures, social media, and other marketing materials.

Collaborate with marketing and product teams to translate concepts into engaging visuals.

Maintain brand consistency throughout all projects and materials.

Review final designs, suggest improvements, and prepare files for production or digital publishing.

Stay updated on design trends and best practices to ensure innovative output.

Required Skills & Qualifications:

Proven experience as a Graphic Designer or in a similar role.

Strong portfolio showcasing expertise in Adobe Creative Suite (Photoshop, Illustrator, InDesign).

Good understanding of typography, color theory, and layout design.

Excellent communication and collaboration skills.

Ability to manage multiple projects and meet deadlines.

Preferred Qualifications:

Degree in Graphic Design, Visual Arts, or related field.

Knowledge of web design and UX/UI principles.
